# Product Context

version: 2026-03-03

## Goals

- Improve onboarding completion for new users.
- Reduce churn for high-usage power users.
- Maintain release quality while increasing iteration speed.

## Vocabulary

- Activation: User completes onboarding and creates first meaningful outcome.
- Power user: Account with high weekly action volume.
- Release impact window: Two weeks post-release before judging adoption effects.
